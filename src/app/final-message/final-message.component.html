My Dearest Farah ❤️

w ehna benwassal le nehayet el advendture, 3ayzak te3raf ad eh enty far2a ma3aya. kol la7za ma3aky 8alya awyyy, w kol yom ma3aky a7la mn ely ablo. enty 8ayarteny lel a7san we 5alteny dyman bafakar lel most2bl we eh ely momkn 23mlo 3shan nb2a m3 b3d we n3esh sawa mabsoten.

You are my best friend, my secret keeper, w my greatest love. baw3edek a7ebek men gher shoroot, asanedek fi kol a7lamek, w akon gambek fi kol wa2t t7tageny fe insha2Allah.

kol sana w enty tayeb ya 7bebet alby. ya rab sneen kter tanya ne3mel fiha zekrayat gamela sawa. ana ba7ebbak aktar mel kalam ma ye2dar ye2ol, w ana momtan awi enek mawgooda fe 7ayaty.

Forever Yours,

Osama ❤️
